English Courses To Become Fluent

Beginner English

A0 - A1

Our Beginner Level course is perfect for young learners who are taking their first steps in learning English. Through engaging activities, interactive games, and captivating storytelling, we introduce children to the fundamental aspects of the English language. They will learn basic vocabulary, simple sentence structures, and essential grammar concepts.

  • Course Includes:
  • Skills Assessment
  • 250+ hours of learning material
  • Fun and Engaging Material
  • Focus on speaking from day 1
  • Colors, numbers, & shapes
  • Grammar
  • Pronounciation & alphabet
  • Basic sentence structure

Intermediate English

A2 - B1

For children who have grasped the basics and are ready to dive deeper, our Intermediate Level course offers a more comprehensive exploration of the English language. Building upon their existing knowledge, students will expand their vocabulary, develop stronger grammatical skills, and improve their reading and writing abilities. Interactive exercises and creative projects will keep them motivated and engaged throughout the learning process.

  • Course Includes:
  • Skills Assessment
  • 250+ hours of learning material
  • Vocabulary tailored to your interests
  • Engaging Speaking & Listening Practice
  • Grammar explained
  • Games
  • Pronounciation & alphabet
  • More advanced sentence structure
Contact Us

Advanced English

B2 - C1

Our Advanced Level course is designed for children who have a solid foundation in English and are ready to take their language skills to the next level. This course focuses on enhancing fluency, expanding vocabulary, and refining grammar and pronunciation. Through stimulating discussions, challenging reading materials, and writing assignments, students will gain confidence and proficiency in expressing themselves effectively in English.

  • Course Includes:
  • Skills Assessment
  • 250+ hours of learning material
  • Vocabulary tailored to your interests
  • Advanced Speaking & Listening Practice
  • Grammar concepts reviewed as needed
  • Speaking and listening games
  • Focus on areas the student has weakness
  • Role Play to solidify listening and speaking skills
